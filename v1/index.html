High-Rise Harvest

A Farming Simulation Raising Awareness of Vertical Farming Practices.

About the Game

Step into a 3D first-person farming simulation. Play as Bobbito, manage a compact vertical farm lot, manage resources, and navigate market stalls to upgrade your setup and support your family.

🏡

Inherit the Farm

Bobbito arrives at his ailing grandfather Lolo Grice's backyard farm. With a letter left behind, he takes on the responsibility of keeping the farm alive.

→
🌱

Grow & Defend

Plant, water, fertilize, and harvest crops across four seasons. Protect your yield from pests, boars, and raccoons using tools from the market.

→
💰

Earn & Progress

Sell your harvest, meet seasonal quotas, and earn enough Shep to pay off Lolo Grice's hospital bills before the final season ends.

Game Features

🌾

Vertical Farming

Maximize crop yields using innovative, space-saving agricultural methods with tiered vertical farm plots.

🐗

Pest Defense

Protect your crops from boars, squirrels, raccoons, and caterpillars using water balloons and pest sprays.

🛒

Dynamic Market

Interact with NPCs to buy seeds, upgrade tools, and sell your harvest at prices that shift with every season.

🍂

Seasonal Progression

Navigate four seasons — Spring, Autumn, Summer, and Winter — each with unique crops and increasing difficulty.

💧

Resource Management

Balance water, energy, and fertilizer to keep crops healthy. Compost spoiled produce into natural fertilizers.

🎒

Inventory System

Manage a five-slot hotbar and storage shed to organize seeds, tools, and harvested crops efficiently.
